📚 Fishing Glossary

🐟 Reading the Water

Fish congregate near structure — rocks, weeds, depth changes. Look for calm pockets in moving water and shaded areas on sunny days.

🌿 Tides & Feeding Times

Many fish feed aggressively 2 hours before and after high tide. Dawn and dusk are also prime feeding windows for most species.

🍖 Catch & Release

Wet your hands before handling fish to protect their slime coat. Keep them in water as long as possible and release headfirst into the current.

🌡 Line & Hook Size

Match gear to your target: 4–6 lb line for panfish, 10–20 lb for bass, and 30–80 lb for larger saltwater species.
